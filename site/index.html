One clear promise

QuickPreview helps you review motion and timing quickly, organize the important moments, and protect sensitive ones without putting a full editor between you and the clip.

Step 01

Load a clip and jump straight in

Open a video, hit play, and start reviewing immediately with keyboard controls that support fast stop-start work.

Step 02

Dial in the exact moment

Scrub, fine-seek, coarse-seek, or frame-step to pinpoint the part of the clip that matters. The player is optimized for repetition and timing work.

Step 03

Loop what matters, then save the exact moment

Repeat the whole file or isolate a segment, then bookmark the important point so you can come back later without losing the review flow.
